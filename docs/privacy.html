Rhythm — Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Overview

Rhythm is a Chrome extension for wellbeing and schedule reminders. This policy describes how it handles your data.

Data Collection

Rhythm collects no user data. No information is ever transmitted, sold, or shared with any third party.

Local Storage Only

All data — including reminder settings, daily completion counts, quick notes, and preferences — is stored exclusively in chrome.storage.local on your own device. It never leaves your browser.

No External Services

Rhythm makes no network requests. It has no analytics, no telemetry, no crash reporting, and no third-party SDKs.

Permissions Used

  • storage — saves your settings and notes locally on your device.
  • alarms — schedules reminder timers using Chrome's alarm API.
  • notifications — shows reminder prompts via the system notification center.
  • offscreen — plays an optional audio chime when a reminder fires (Web Audio API requires an offscreen document in Manifest V3).
